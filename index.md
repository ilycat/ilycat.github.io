<h2>An island with no shore and a single tree</h2>

All the hair we lose  
where does it go  
Yours is still in my room somewhere  
I suppose  
moon rocks  






All the love we give  
It pours  
Rain in Santander  

Scary it might go  
down the drain  
and back  

Let’s water our plants  
The shadows grow in size  
All of that sun  
is ours  
to share
